title: Electronic queues in public-facing services
description: Design constraints for clinics, permit offices, and citizen service centers—high traffic, strict fairness, and accessibility.
date: '2026-04-08'
---

## Citizens expect clarity, not clever UX tricks

Public queues need obvious states: where you are in line, how long you might wait, and what to do if you step away.

## Accessibility and multilingual support

Touch targets, contrast, and simple language matter more than animation. Plan for at least the languages your frontline staff already supports.

## Reporting without over-collecting

Operational dashboards should answer supervisor questions (peak load, SLA breaches) without storing unnecessary personal data.

## Waiting time is not automatically staff time

Visitors waiting does not map one-to-one to FTE savings. Some waiting is structural (peak arrivals); some friction is policy or staffing rules.

## Build an internal model your CFO can stress-test

Separate **visitor experience** metrics (median wait, abandonment) from **staff workload** proxies (manual reconciliations, escalations). Tie any savings claim to a scenario your operations lead signs off on.

## Use calculators as discussion tools

Interactive ROI widgets are useful for alignment—not as guarantees. Always pair numbers with explicit assumptions and ranges.

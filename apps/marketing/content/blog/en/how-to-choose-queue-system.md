## Start from the workflow, not the brochure

Before comparing logos, write down what actually happens today: how visitors join a queue, how staff calls the next person, and what reports you need weekly.

## Five questions worth asking any vendor

1. **What happens when the network drops?** Kiosks and counters should degrade predictably.
2. **How fast can a new branch go live?** Same-day configuration beats a six-week project plan.
3. **Where does data live—and who can export it?** Align answers with your security review early.
4. **Which integrations are productized versus custom?** Calendars, SMS, and identity are common blockers.
5. **How will you measure success after go-live?** Waiting time, abandonment, and staff touches per visitor are good starting metrics.
